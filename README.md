I went there last year.\n- A. Have; been  \n- B. Have; gone  \n- C. Do; go  \n- D. Did; go\n\n---\n\n<div style=\"min-height: 80px; border-bottom: 1px dashed #ccc; margin-bottom: 16px;\"></div>\n\n---\n\n> **答案：** A  \n> **解析：** 根据答语‘Yes, I have.’可知问句是现在完成时，have been to表示‘去过某地’，have gone to表示‘去了某地还没回来’，这里表示去过北京，用have been to。\n\n### 第 3 题（3 分）\n—______? —It's sunny and warm.\n- A. How's the weather like  \n- B. What's the weather  \n- C. How's the weather  \n- D. What's the weather look like\n\n---\n\n<div style=\"min-height: 80px; border-bottom: 1px dashed #ccc; margin-bottom: 16px;\"></div>\n\n---\n\n> **答案：** C  \n> **解析：** 询问天气常用‘How's the weather?’或‘What's the weather like?’。\n\n### 第 4 题（3 分）\n—What does your father do? —He is a ______. He works in a hospital.\n- A. teacher  \n- B. doctor  \n- C. farmer  \n- D. driver\n\n---\n\n<div style=\"min-height: 80px; border-bottom: 1px dashed #ccc; margin-bottom: 16px;\"></div>\n\n---\n\n> **答案：** B  \n> **解析：** 根据‘He works in a hospital.’可知他是一名医生，doctor意为‘医生’。\n\n### 第 5 题（3 分）\n—______ is your schoolbag? —It's blue.\n- A. What color  \n- B. What size  \n- C. How much  \n- D. How many\n\n---\n\n<div style=\"min-height: 80px; border-bottom: 1px dashed #ccc; margin-bottom: 16px;\"></div>\n\n---\n\n> **答案：** A  \n> **解析：** 根据答语‘It's blue.’可知问的是书包的颜色，what color意为‘什么颜色’。\n\n### 第 6 题（3 分）\n—______? —I'd like a glass of milk.\n- A. What would you like to drink  \n- B. What would you like to eat  \n- C. How about some milk  \n- D. Do you like milk\n\n---\n\n<div style=\"min-height: 80px; border-bottom: 1px dashed #ccc; margin-bottom: 16px;\"></div>\n\n---\n\n> **答案：** A  \n> **解析：** 根据答语‘I'd like a glass of milk.’可知问的是想喝什么，what would you like to drink意为‘你想喝什么’。\n\n### 第 7 题（3 分）\n—______ is your mother? —She is a nurse.\n- A. What  \n- B. Who  \n- C. How  \n- D. Where\n\n---\n\n<div style=\"min-height: 80px; border-bottom: 1px dashed #ccc; margin-bottom: 16px;\"></div>\n\n---\n\n> **答案：** A  \n> **解析：** 询问职业用‘What is sb.?’。\n\n### 第 8 题（3 分）\n—______ is it from your home to school? —It's about 2 kilometers.\n- A. How far  \n- B. How long  \n- C. How often  \n- D. How soon\n\n---\n\n<div style=\"min-height: 80px; border-bottom: 1px dashed #ccc; margin-bottom: 16px;\"></div>\n\n---\n\n> **答案：** A  \n> **解析：** 根据答语‘It's about 2 kilometers.’可知问的是距离，how far意为‘多远’。\n\n### 第 9 题（3 分）\n—______? —It's 8:30.\n- A. What time is it  \n- B. What's the date  \n- C. What day is it  \n- D. How old are you\n\n---\n\n<div style=\"min-height: 80px; border-bottom: 1px dashed #ccc; margin-bottom: 16px;\"></div>\n\n---\n\n> **答案：** A  \n> **解析：** 根据答语‘It's 8:30.’可知问的是时间，what time is it意为‘几点了’。\n\n### 第 10 题（3 分）\n—______ is your favorite subject? —English.\n- A. What  \n- B. Who  \n- C. How  \n- D. Why\n\n---\n\n<div style=\"min-height: 80px; border-bottom: 1px dashed #ccc; margin-bottom: 16px;\"></div>\n\n---\n\n> **答案：** A  \n> **解析：** 根据答语‘English.’可知问的是最喜欢的科目，用what。\n\n---\n**试卷到此结束**
